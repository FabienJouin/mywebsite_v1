About myself


My name is Fabien Jouin a Berlin based interactive designer.
In 2007 I gratuated from "l'ecole Pivaut" in Nantes with a visual communication degree. I have worked for several agencies like Ocedille, Les arbres fruitiers and iconmobile GmbH. I got a wide range of experience from graphic design to User Interface design.
From my last 2 years of work experience I have developed a passion for digital design and technology. I also adapt the ability to work collaboratively with developers. I really enjoy creating beautiful, functional and pixels perfect User Interface. My portfolio is a mix of created independently work and as part of an agency.

My Tools


For each project you need the right tools.
I got lot of experiences with Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign) I work on print design layout and User Interface. I also work with Keynote or powerpoint when I need to create stunny presentation. Lately I taught myself to learn HTML and CSS.
